1.Choose the location in google map
2.Find the share option. Choose embed tag in share.
3.Copy the iframe link and paste it in your html file. Now you can view the map in your result.
4.To make map responsive place the iframe inside the div tag. And then make the position of top and left of the div tag as absolute. 
And make the iframe position as relative in style/css file.
5.You can change the height and the width of the map by changing the height and width of the iframe tag
